OUR MANAGEMENT

Dr. Sreedhar Mayavan, Ph.D (Bioch)

Dr. Sreedhar Mayavan leads his team to provide expert guidance that helps the clinician to determine the best treatment for every individual patient. With an experience of over two decades and equipped with skills at the highest level, he immensely contributes to the technological progress in the field of Prenatal and Neonatal Diagnosis.

He has been instrumental to set up the first comprehensive prenatal screening lab in India. His achievement speaks volumes about his experience, skill, expertise and knowledge in the field of speciality diagnostics. His constant quest for excellence and perfection has driven him to set up PrimeGen Healthcare Laboratories Private Limited with a mission to provide results of international standard at the highest quality.
